Our Story

What started as a small family ice cream shop has grown into a beloved brand that serves communities nationwide.

The Beginning

In 2010, the Patel family opened their first ice cream shop with a simple mission: to create the most delicious ice cream using only the finest ingredients. What began as a small corner shop has now become a household name.
